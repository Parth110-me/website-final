About Us

Blush & Bloom Beauty Studio is a premium ladies salon nestled in the heart of Dum Dum, Kolkata. We offer a tranquil, luxurious space where every woman is treated with elegance and care. From expert hair styling to rejuvenating skin treatments and bridal transformations, our skilled team ensures you leave glowing inside and out. Experience beauty tailored just for you – where tradition meets trend.
